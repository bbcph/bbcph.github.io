Our Story

A young lady that loves art specifically painting. Since, painting is a pricey hobby she used to bought basic colors of paint and mixed it to create another color where she can put and used on her paintings. On her cousin's high school graduation there is no one's available to make over her cousins that's why Mae was asked if she can do it for her cousins. Even though hesitating because it's her first time and the cosmetics available isn't right for the skin color of her cousin she used her skills in painting and did make over her cousin like her artworks which turns actuality good for first timer.

From that, Mae's cousin noticed her skills where they used it to starting a research, test and develop a cosmetic products that will match for all skin tones. With efforts and successful results they started a cosmetic business that became bloomed and went viral by the word of mouth and social media spread the efficacy of the beauty berries cosmetics product flourished.

Beauty Berries Cosmetics is a cosmetics brand in the Philippines that launched on March 10,2021 by Mae Paghubasan.

Vision

Everyone is already a beautiful piece of nature and cosmetics is just a piece of artwork for people to have fun with.

Mission

BBC wants society to embrace their skintone without going fairer and darker tones to fit into the traditional color tones. Also, to inspire everyone that they can be beyond whatever they imagined to be.
